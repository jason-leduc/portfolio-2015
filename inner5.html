Crazy Horse Logo Re-design

I chose to redesign the worst loking logo in my professional opinion. This logo was to be of a small business that is located in Canada. It could not be a national corporation or well-known industry or business. I wanted to find one that I could really sink my teeth into and create a dynamic design.

My Personal Objectives

I wanted to demonstrate a variety of skills when redesigning this logo. I wanted to show my creative thinking process with original concepts, my knowledge and ability to work through the stages of designing a logo, my know-how about producinging a logo that can be used with any medium and size, and my proficiency in developinging a logo within a 2 colour parameter.
