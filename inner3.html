Quote Booklet: Red Hot Chili Peppers

I was tasked to design a 16-page self-cover booklet using only TYPE. Some sort of hand-made type aspect had to be included in the booklet in order to contrast the hand made with only ONE type family.

My objectives for this project...

...to explore type as image and effectively use creative typography. Create visual flow and consistency throughout a document. Design with provided specifications and guidelines. Use grid systems and advanced layout techniques. Build properly composed InDesign files. Refine creative and technical publication design skills.

Theme and Content

The theme I chose is the the band Red Hot Chili Peppers. I chose this band specifically because they are my all time favourite musical group and I thought it would be cool to create a small promo piece.

As for the content of my piece, I wanted to include a mixture of their most well known songs and some of my personal favourites.

Hand-made Type

Because they are known as the Red Hot Chili Peppers, I thought that it would be appropriate to create a typeface out of chili peppers. I did this by arranging the peppers into the shapes of letters and then editing the photos in Photoshop. I had to do quite a bit of editing... I arranged the peppers letter by letter and photographed each one. I then spelled out the song titles using the indivdual characters. I also had to colour correct the peppers because when I bought them they were originally green. The front and back covers for the booklet were made in Illustrator using simple polygons.
